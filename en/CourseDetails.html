Salary Preparation, Analysis, and Management

Edit View

Employee costs in terms of salaries and other benefits are among the largest costs for any company, alongside the cost of goods sold (cost of sales), rent, and depreciation. If you work for a goods-producing company, this cost is part of the cost of merchandise or part of operating costs, which has a dual impact on you. Properly handling and reporting these costs is key to analysis and decision-making. This course begins with an introduction to the departments of human resources, accounting, finance, and payroll. It will cover the preparation and calculation of salaries, including the necessary controls and matters to verify in advance to ensure data is used correctly.

Methodology:

The consultant will present a presentation and examples. Participants will be asked to perform exercises on the payroll and benefits Excel program, in addition to group workshops and presentations.

Course Goals:

Participants in the course will be able to:
  • Understand the tasks of human resources, accounting, and finance and how to contribute to their success.
  • Prepare and accurately review monthly payrolls.
  • Calculate employee benefits, loans, vacations, and end-of-service gratuities.
  • Prepare monthly and annual payroll budgets.
  • Match payroll statements and audit supporting documents for payment.
  • Design periodic reports for payroll management and analysis.

Target Groups:

Employees, accountants, assistant accountants, supervisors, managers responsible for payroll, and HR specialists interested in understanding and applying best practices related to payroll department management.

Target Competencies:

  • Monitor all employees.
  • Handle payroll accounting and reconciliations.
  • Prepare and process payrolls.
  • Prepare payroll budgets and expenses.
  • Analyze payrolls and prepare reports.
  • Balance the expenses and assets department.
